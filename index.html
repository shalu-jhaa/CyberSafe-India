Online Scams

Deceptive schemes to trick victims into sending money or revealing financial information.

Malware

Identity Theft

Protection Strategies

Essential practices to keep your digital life secure

Create Strong Passwords

Weak passwords are the easiest way for hackers to access your accounts. Follow these guidelines:

  • Use at least 12 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid common words, phrases, or personal information
  • Use a unique password for each account
  • Consider using a passphrase (e.g., "CorrectHorseBatteryStaple")

Secure Your Devices

Protect all your devices from malware and unauthorized access:

Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your operating system and apps to patch security vulnerabilities.

Use Antivirus Software

Install reputable antivirus software and keep it updated for real-time protection.

Secure Your Network

Use WPA3 encryption for your Wi-Fi and change default router passwords.

Backup Your Data

Regularly backup important files to an external drive or cloud storage.

Financial Safety Online

Protect your money and financial information from digital threats:

Banking Safety Tips

  • Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone
  • Verify website URLs before entering login details
  • Use official banking apps instead of mobile browsers
  • Enable transaction alerts for all account activity
  • Check statements regularly for unauthorized transactions

UPI Payment Scams

Beware of fake customer care numbers, QR code scams, and requests for screen sharing. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone.
